The Blood Donation Process

1

Registration & Health Check

Complete health questionnaire and mini-physical exam to ensure donation safety.

2

Blood Collection

Actual donation takes 10-15 minutes. You'll donate 350-450ml of blood.

3

Recovery & Refreshments

Rest for 15 minutes with snacks and drinks to ensure you feel well before leaving.
